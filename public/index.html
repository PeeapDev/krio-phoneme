Krio Phoneme & Pronunciation

Phase 1 · single-speaker · rule-based · no AI

1. Phoneme Registry

Single speaker, clean environment, one recording per phoneme. Files saved as phoneme_<key>.wav.

—

2. Lexicon

Strict pipeline: lowercase a–z plus ɛ ɔ only · single word (no spaces, no hyphens) · every phoneme must exist in registry and have an audio recording · syllables must fit (C){0,3} V (C){0,2}.
Bulk imports never overwrite — duplicates are skipped.

▸ Bulk import (paste many words)

One word per line. Optional gloss after = or | or tab. Lines starting with # are ignored. Examples: kushe = hello, chɔp | food, bɛd.
